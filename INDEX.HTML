This privacy policy governs your use of the SANJU LOGISTICS mobile application ("App") and describes how we collect, use, and share your personal information.

Information We Collect

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following cases:

Data Security

We use appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Data Retention

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to provide services. Booking and payment data may be retained longer for legal and business purposes.

Your Rights

  • Correct inaccurate data
